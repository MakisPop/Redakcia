Escape from Tarkov is a hardcore and realistic online 
person-first action Simulator/RPG with MMO features and 
a driven-story walkthrough.
----
With each passing day the situation in the Norvinsk 
region grows more and more complicated. Incessant warfare in 
Tarkov has sparked massive panic; 
----
Tarkov is sealed off by UN and Russian military, 
supply chains are cut, communication with operational 
command is lost.
